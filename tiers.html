Basic

Dreamer can decide the landscapes/objects they wish to experience. (There are no people interactions in their dream and they have no control whilst dreaming.)

Standard

Dreamer can decide the people and landscapes/objects they wish to experience. (They have no control whilst dreaming.)

Premium

Dreamer has full control whilst dreaming and can decide the people and landscapes/objects they wish to experience.

Premium Plus (Coming Soon...)

Multiple dreamers can share one dream and interact with full control.
